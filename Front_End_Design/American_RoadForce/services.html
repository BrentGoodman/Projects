Our Services

What we can do for you

Lift Kits

A lift kit is a vehicle modification that lifts either the body or the suspension to give the vehicle a high profile appearance and increases performance and ground clearance.

Tires / Wheels

The wheel is the circular metal object composed of the rim and the disc that serves as a support to the tire, whereas the tire is the ring-shaped rubber object attached around the wheel that protects the rim.

Suspension

The job of a car suspension is to maximize the friction between the tires and the road surface, to provide steering stability with good handling and to ensure the comfort of the passengers.

Alignments

A wheel alignment consists of adjusting the angles of the wheels so that they are perpendicular to the ground and parallel to each other.

TPMS

Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is an electronic system in your vehicle that monitors your tire air pressure and alerts you when it falls dangerously low.
